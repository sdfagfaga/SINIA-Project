FREQUESTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I ship products to my friends and relatives?

Yes, you can buy products for other people. However, allow them to sample the quality from the advertising provided by the vendor. This, to ensure your friends and family have a positive experience. Can I buy products more than 100 miles away Our goal is to have you purchase high quality products with great value. In buying a product that is over 100 miles away, you are likely to incur additional shipping costs that increase your purchase price and affect your product's value>

hIs shipping included in the price?

No, shipping is an added cost to you that is paid directly and separately by Siniia to the shipping agent. We have provided shippers with reasonable prices to lower your costs. Please select the option to obtain other shippers or obtain reduced shipping by pooling with other buyers. With this option, the product may take longer to reach you.

Can I purchase products from other countries?

Yes, please first ensure that you are able to purchase products from the vendor without steep taxes and tariffs.

Can I open an account for a standing order for every week or Month?

You are welcome to opening a standing order of which the vendor can ship items to you as frequently as possible. However, your payment must be processed the day your product is set to be shipped.

If I know my vendor, can I contact them directly?

Once you are familiar with the vendor, you may contact them directly. Our goal is to set up a network for your well-being. We will miss you when you go, keep in touch.
